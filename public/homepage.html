About Songhai Health Trust

Since 2001, Songhai Health Trust has been a pioneer in Nigeria's healthcare insurance industry, committed to providing accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare coverage.

  • First-generation HMO licensed by NHIS
  • Over 500 accredited healthcare providers nationwide
  • 24/7 customer support and call centre
  • Digital-first approach with proprietary software platform
  • Transparent claims processing and management
